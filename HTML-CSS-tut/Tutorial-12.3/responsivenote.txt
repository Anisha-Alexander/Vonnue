What is a responsive site?
__________________________

- People view websites in many different devices

-Make the site easyb to use and easy on the eye, no matter which device it is viewed on 

-Therefore we make our websites 'responsive' , meaning the design responds to the specification of the device


Responsive from the offset !
_____________________________

-Responsive design shouldn't be an afterthrough or a side order tp your desktop website.

-Think about responsive design from the very start

-plan our content efficiently with all devices in mind.
 


 Importance !
 ___________
 
 -Tons of new devices coming out which allows users to browse websites.

 -No longer the 'norm' to view a site on a desktop.

 -If you dont make your websites very mobile friendly, you'll miss out on alot of viewers.
